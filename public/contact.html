Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. We do not solicit users for personal information and we do not share personal information with any third parties. The only information required to submit a contact form is the senders name and message. Providing a last name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information is entirely optional. Users who provide their phone number and/or email address may receive phone calls and/or emails from Ace Teppanyaki. Ace Teppanyaki only stores user-provided information for customer service and customer satisfaction purposes. User-provided information will not be used for marketing, promotional, or advertising purposes. User-provided information will not be shared with anyone outside of Ace Teppanyaki.
